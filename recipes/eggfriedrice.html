Egg Fried Rice

Home

Description

Simple and classic egg fried rice.

Ingredients

  • Jasmine rice
  • Three Eggs
  • Spring Onions
  • Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • MSG
  • White Pepper

Method

  1. Cook the rice. Meanwhile, whisk the eggs in a bowl.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan, over a high heat. Meanwhile, slice the spring onion.
  3. Once hot, add the eggs to the pan to cook.
  4. Add 1tsp sugar, MSG, white pepper, stir.
  5. Add 2tbsp soy sauce, stir.
  6. Serve, sprinkle over chopped spring onion.
