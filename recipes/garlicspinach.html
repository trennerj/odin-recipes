Garlic Spinach

Description

A fresh and simple side veg dish. Sauteed spinach with garlic and a dash of soy is quick and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic (2 Cloves)

Method

  1. Heat a small amount of oil on a medium-low heat.
  2. Saute√© the spinach until wilted. Meanwhile, peel and thinly slice the garlic.
  3. Once the spinach has wilted, add the garlic.
  4. Once the garlic is fragrant, add a dash of soy sauce, and stir.
  5. Serve.
